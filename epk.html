Biography

Straight-up rock n' roll with a biting humor and a Wasatch Front grit.

Based in Salt Lake City, Buster brings a raw energy to the stage, fueled by the unique culture they grew up in. Their music is a high-octane blend of heavy riffs and satirical wit, aimed directly at the heart of the Utah experience.

The band consists of Wes Furgason (drums/professional cat herder), Karl Gilchrist (guitar/vocals/possibly the illegitimate son of Jim Morrison), Dave Call (bass/Snack Master), and Jon Beutler (lead guitar/The Professor). Together, they are Busterâ€”and they are here to rock you.

Stage Requirements

  • Monitors: 4 discrete mixes. Wes prefer s the drum monitor positioned on his Left Side. The other three monitors can be placed Left, Center, and Right.
  • Power: Individual power drops (quad-boxes) required at each member's station: Front Center (Karl), Stage Right (Dave), Stage Left (Jon), and Drum Riser (Wes).
  • Drum Kit: Wes plays a 5-piece kit.
